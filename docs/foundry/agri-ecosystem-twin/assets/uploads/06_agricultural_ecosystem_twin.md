# Agricultural Ecosystem Twin

## One-line vision

A digital twin platform for farms, regenerative agriculture, soil systems, biodiversity, crop planning, and ecological interventions.

## Core thesis

Agriculture is a complex state-changing system. Most farm software manages operations, compliance, or yield data. Few products model the causal relationship between soil health, biodiversity, weather, interventions, and long-term resilience.

The opportunity is to build an ecosystem twin that helps farmers and advisors plan interventions with projected effects.

## Killer feature

> “What is the likely effect of adding cover crop mix A versus B on soil moisture, pest pressure, and yield over three seasons?”

## MVP

### MVP segment

### MVP scope

- plot map
- soil indicators

- advisor report

### MVP promise

> Turn regenerative practices into measurable, comparable, compounding intervention plans.

## Differentiation via Substrate

- Nested subjects: plot → field → farm → watershed
- Plan recursion: year → season → intervention sequence
- Flat observations: soil tests, weather, yield
- Effect declarations: expected impact of interventions
- Catalog flywheel: learn which practices work under which conditions

## Risks

- Data sparsity
- farmer software adoption barriers
- long feedback loops
- weather confounding
- fragmented regional practices

## Strategic rating

Strong conceptual fit and attractive long-term market, but slower feedback loops make it less ideal than AI/organization verticals for fast product learning.
